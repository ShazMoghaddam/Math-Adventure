Privacy Policy

Math Adventure  ·  Last updated:

🌟 The short version (for parents)

  • Math Adventure does not collect any personal information
  • No accounts, no sign-ups, no names or emails required
  • No adverts, no in-app purchases, no third-party tracking
  • Game progress is saved only on your device
  • The game works fully offline once loaded

1. Who we are

Math Adventure is an educational maths game designed for children aged 4–9. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to the developers of Math Adventure.

2. What information we collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever.

The only data stored by Math Adventure is your child's in-game progress (number of carrots earned, worlds unlocked, and star ratings). This data is stored exclusively using your device's local storage — it never leaves the device and is never transmitted to any server.

Math Adventure has no servers, no accounts, no logins, and no network communication of any kind during gameplay.

3. Children's privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Math Adventure is designed for children. We take this responsibility seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from any user, including children under 13 (US) or under 16 (EU/UK).
  • No parental consent is required because no data is collected.
  • There are no behavioural analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party data processors embedded in the game.

4. Third-party services

Math Adventure loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This request is made when the game first loads and may reveal your IP address to Google. After the first load, fonts are cached on your device and no further requests are made. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Service Purpose Data shared Optional?
Google Fonts Display game fonts IP address (on first load only) No (cached after first load)

5. Local storage

Math Adventure uses your browser's localStorage to save game progress between sessions. This data is stored only on your device. It contains:

  • Number of carrots earned
  • Which worlds have been unlocked
  • Star ratings per world

You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data, or (on mobile) by deleting and reinstalling the app.

6. Advertising

Math Adventure contains no advertising of any kind. No ad networks, no sponsored content, no tracking pixels.

7. In-app purchases

Math Adventure contains no in-app purchases. All content is available for free with no paywalls.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy — for example, if we add new features — we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection without clearly informing users and, where required by law, obtaining consent.
